# Privacy Policy for RFAntennaCalc

**Last updated: November 14, 2025**

## Overview

RFAntennaCalc ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

## Information Collection and Use

**RFAntennaCalc does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or data.**

### What We Don't Collect
- No personal information (name, email, phone number)
- No location data
- No device identifiers
- No usage statistics or analytics
- No crash reports
- No cookies or tracking technologies

### How the App Works
All antenna calculations are performed **locally on your device**. The App:
- Does not require an internet connection
- Does not communicate with any servers
- Does not store any data beyond your current session
- Does not share any information with third parties

## Data Storage

The App may temporarily store your input values (frequencies, lengths, velocity factors) in your device's memory during use. This data:
- Remains only on your device
- Is never transmitted anywhere
- Is cleared when you close the app
- Is not backed up to iCloud or any cloud service

## Third-Party Services

RFAntennaCalc does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

## Children's Privacy

RFAntennaCalc does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal information to:
- Access
- Correct
- Delete
- Export
